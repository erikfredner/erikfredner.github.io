One of the main characters in my dissertation is the "average man."
Something that fascinates me about this concept-character is the
particularity of the average—-they are not "normal," "ordinary,"
"everyday," etc. but resolutely "average." There's much to be said about the prevalence of and
preference for this idea in the period I'm studying, but for the moment I want to distinguish (as
much for myself as anyone else) the range of terms that hover around
this means of representing centrality.
