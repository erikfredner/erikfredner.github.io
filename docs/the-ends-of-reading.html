The Ends of Reading

New analyses of US government survey data reveal that people in the US are far less likely to read for pleasure than they were a few decades ago. And the decline in reading for pleasure may be accelerating among children. This matters for literary studies because reading for pleasure is an unacknowledged prerequisite for the discipline.
